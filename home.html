The Meme "Beluga"

Beluga cat is a black and white cat with a surprised expression that has become a viral sensation on the internet. The cat's real name is not known, but it has been nicknamed "Beluga" due to its resemblance to the Beluga whale. The nickname caught on after a Twitter user posted a picture of the cat with the caption "Beluga cat is always surprised to see you". Since then, the cat has been widely known as "Beluga cat" and has been featured in numerous memes and social media posts.
